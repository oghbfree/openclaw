My Testimonial:

Some time ago, I developed a severe inflamed colon (Colitis). The condition and pain were so awful that I looked ashen and grey! I couldn't eat my 'normal' cooked Caribbean and Indian foods without experiencing some kind of pain in my gut afterwards, which could last all night. I would graze on dry bland crackers or dry foods to briefly abate the pain. In short I was miserable. Even raw vegetables caused great discomfort at that time.

In the end I went to see a naturopath for a diagnosis and who understood the power of healing nutrition, in particular, raw plant based food. He advised me to consume green juices only for one month. No eating. I was desperate to be pain free at that point and so was willing to try anything natural and reasonable. I was past the point of hunger being an issue.

I did not want to visit a doctor, because although they may well have diagnosed what was wrong, I did not want to take drugs to ease the symptoms only and which could also harm my body system at a cellular level. I also wanted to address the causes and irradiate any chance of re-occurence.

I juiced everything I could get my hands on, green leaves, broccoli, green beans, celery, courgette, parsley, cucumber, kale, lemon etc and not too much fruit. Working full time, my daughter would make and bring me juices at lunch time and I would have a juice waiting for me when I returned from work. I cannot emphasis enough that for me, guidance from a professional and practical family support during this healing process were paramount to my success.

After 5 days, all pain was gone and I began feeling well. By ten days, my energy levels soared and I was glowing. I didn't feel weak, but completely rejuvenated and renewed. My appetite was restored and by the end of 30 days, I began introducing light salads and gentle soups. That was the beginning of my raw.

Why am I telling you this?

Based on my own personal experience, the testimonials I'm aware of, the numerous lectures and training courses I've attended, books and studies I've read, individuals I've coached, cleanses I've undertaken, I am totally convinced of the healing and transformational power of raw plant and living food...

Since committing to a healthier lifestyle, I know that eating nutrient dense, fresh, organic, raw and living food, physical activity (in my case dance and yoga) and spiritual practice, has been the way to maintain my overall sense of wellbeing. Whenever I stray from the path of health, for whatever reason, it doesn't take too long before I begin to recognise the familiar sluggishness resulting from less healthy choices...."Eat Raw & Prosper" —Nasiche McKenzie.
