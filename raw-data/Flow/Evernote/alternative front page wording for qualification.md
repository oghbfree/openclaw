Not sure which qualification or entry level is right for you? This
section gives you an overview of all the qualifications on offer to
you making your decision process easier.
Whatever stage you are at in your career, our qualifications can help
you to develop your sales & marketing skills & gain a broad
understanding of significant issues relating to sales, marketing,
retail management and international business. And because our
qualifications are developed to meet industry needs, they will provide
you with also develop practical skills that can help you to progress,
whilst ensuring that you exercise leadership to initiate and support
issues relating to your field of speciality including your
responsibility to uphold ethical standards..
Whilst each can be taken as a recognised qualification in it's own
right, they can also be taken as an excellent stepping stone to the
corresponding sales & marketing based qualifications.
