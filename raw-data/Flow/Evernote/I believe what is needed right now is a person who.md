I believe what is needed right now is a person who is versatile and who can adapt to changing situations. As well as being versatile there is a need to maintain what the IAM has.

Over my time with the IAM my role has evolved from being a centre account executive which is my job title.

When I started work here with the IAM, there were 4 qualifications on offer. Now there are 39 (excluding IT [Information technology] and endorsed qualifications which brings the total number up to 54).

Qualification administration is processed at the moment via Rory and I with very minimal errors.

Further Education

With the slow take up of IAM courses within the FE market, the Tier 4 market remains to be our main money earner. I have good knowledge of Tier 4 centres and good relationships with local and international centres.

Tier 4 USP’s

- Level 6 Extended Diploma. Combined level 4, 5 & 6 qualification. Three year visa, one CAS.
- Choose between exams or written assignments
- Assignments are marked by the centre, moderated by the IAM
- Four exam/moderation series per year (March, June, September, December)
- Programme endorsement opportunities
- Professional membership (tutor and student)
- UKBA Highly Trusted Status (HTS) success rate. We actively support our centres during QAA and ISI inspections, experiencing a 90% success rate*
- Seamless transfers and exemptions
- Discounts for overseas or multiple accreditations
- Credit certificates
- 15 working day turnaround

*correct as of 26th June 2012.

We can talk to or existing centres to encourage more registrations and in turn exam entries.
