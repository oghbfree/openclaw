The past is a ghost, the future a dream and all we ever have is now.”

1. Live in the Moment. Learn from mistakes, but move on quickly. Enjoy where you are NOW, with an eye on where you want to be tomorrow. Every breath is a blessing, and every moment is a milestone.

2. Push Yourself with Passion. No one should ask more of you than you do of yourself. Set a high bar, and when you reach it, move it higher. Act with urgency. Breathe fire. The right Attitude and Energy can overcome anything.

3. Surround Yourself with Positive People. There are only 2 types of people in the world, those that bring you Up, and those that bring you down. We can’t control everything, but we can damn sure control the company we keep. There is NOTHING more contagious than the energy of those around you.

4. Work hard, Play hard. Work harder. Life is about balance. You need to bust your ass. Then reward yourself. Pat yourself on the back. Do a keg stand. Then work EVEN HARDER. Never stop.

5. Schedule Your Success. Make good things a habit. Make generosity a ritual. Set your alarm clock to learn. When you make productive things a part of your routine, there is NO DEBATE. There is no question. When someone asks you, “Do you REALLY need to workout today?”… You say, “Hell. Yes.”

give GRATITUDE. At least 2 to 3 times a day I stop and give thanks for things that I have in my life; friends, family, career, life itself

Always say thank you. Even the smallest act represents a sacrifice of someone’s time and energy.

Trust you instinct and don’t be afraid to let your passion show. A passionate vision and an actionable plan are an unbeatable combination.
