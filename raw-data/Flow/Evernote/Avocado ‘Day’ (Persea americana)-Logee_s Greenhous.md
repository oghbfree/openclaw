|  Detailed Description |
| **Avocado ‘Day’ (Persea americana)**<br>Our ‘Day’ avocado is by far the easiest avocado to fruit in a pot. Plants will fruit at about 3 feet in height and will produce a medium-sized tapered-neck avocado that is easy to peel and has a delicious, buttery sweet taste. The fruit will hold on the plant for six months with ripening occurring from July to September. Another plus for ‘Day’ is its cold tolerance taking temperatures down into the low twenties. We sell grafted plants that will start bearing fruit in 2 to 3 years.<br>**Hardy to Zone 9 and higher for outdoors.**<br>Full sun, grows to 4-6' in container, minimum temperature indoors 40°, Spring bloomer.<br>[Persea Plant Care](http://www.logees.com/ftg/Persea.pdf) |
