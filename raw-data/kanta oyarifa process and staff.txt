Adding context from another chat: Based on your requirements, here's a structured daily workflow and best practices guide for Staff Member 1:

This is from another deep eek### Core Workflow & Best Practices
1. Opening Routine (Morning)
   * Sweep floors, dust items, check item organization.
   * Inspect tools (cutting discs, etc.) for rust; apply light oil if needed.
   * Review new stock and integrate logically with existing items.

2. Customer Engagement (Ongoing)
   * Browsing Customers: Politely engage ("Finding everything okay?"), offer new stock/discount info, and capture Name/WhatsApp # for updates. *Add immediately to Google Contacts (2realenterprisesgh).*
   * Purchasing Customers: Log ALL sales in Zobase POS with:
     * Full name
     * WhatsApp #
     * Items purchased
     * Invoice/receipt issued
   * Sync: Daily sync Zobase contacts → Google Phonebook.

3. WhatsApp Management (Critical!)
   * Status Updates: Post 5+ new/promoted items with prices by 10 AM daily. Use clear photos + emojis (e.g., "🔥 Drill Bits - ₵15! 🔥").
   * Response Time: Acknowledge all messages/calls within 30 min (beat the 1-hour target). Use quick replies:
     * "Thanks for messaging! Confirming stock for you..."
     * "I’ll check pricing and WhatsApp you shortly."
   * Logging: Note customer queries in Zobase "Remarks" for follow-up.

4. Stock & Maintenance (Afternoon)
   * Verify new stock is displayed, priced, and logged in Zobase.
   * Re-oil tools prone to rust (e.g., cutting discs) if humid/day-end.
   * Quick tidy-up: Reorganize displaced items, sweep high-traffic areas.

5. Closing Routine
   * Final Zobase sync to Google Contacts.
   * Ensure all customer data from the day is captured.
   * Light dusting/sweeping, secure tools.
