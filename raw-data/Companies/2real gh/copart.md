1) 2 x Company/Business trade invoices clearly showing the FULL Company Name and overseas based exporting business address. (These must be less than 3 months old; not-handwritten/office typed and translated into English, please).

2) Copies of any Overseas based ‘business entity/registration’ licenses clearly showing the business name (translated into English, please).

3) Copies of any overseas registered waste management licenses (if held) clearly showing the business name (copy of the original front page, please).

4) Copy Photo I.D. (passport/Personal I.D. or Driving License) for each individual on the account (including yourself if you’re going to be the UK contact).

5) A ‘copy-from-original’ Company/Business Utility Bill or Bank statement dated within the last three months; addressed to the company. (e.g : Utility bill examples : gas, electric, water, TV licence or landline phone). 

6) £60 registration Fee (contact our Member Services Team on 0844 8758837 as they can take a Telephone payment).
