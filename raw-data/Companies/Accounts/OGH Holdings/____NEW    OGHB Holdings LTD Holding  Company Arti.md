OGHB Holdings LTD Holding  Company Articles of Organization 


This is a template for organizing a YouTube Channel Holding Company for the management and ownership of subsidiaries ( Child LLCs) such as operating companies and property management companies.


Very standard and will fit most cases.


1. The name of this parent company is - OGHB Holdings LTD .


2. OGHB Holdings LTD - is organized in England & Wales


3. OGHB Holdings LTD will lawfully operate perpetually in England & Wales. 


4. OGHB Holdings LTD is the holding and management company for the future  subsidiaries  - the operating company  2 real enterprises ltd, OGH Solutions Ltd and future child LTD's


5. OGHB Holdings LTD  grants the subsidiaries current/future broad powers to operate in good faith on its behalf.


6. OGHB Holdings LTD will be the registered agent of future subsidiaries 


7.OGHB Holdings LTD  - Suite 213, 1 Limes Avenue, London, SE20 8QR


8. OGHB Holdings LTD is the registered agent of itself and future child LLCs


9. OGHB Holdings LTD has 1000 shares divided percentage wise Oman Ghan Herbert-Blankson 95% Kobena Robert Herbert-Blankson 5%  and Nenyi Essel Herbert-Blankson 5%


10. Kobena Robert Herbert-Blankson and Nenyi Essel Herbert-Blankson will have not voting rights or any management of the company until ages 25 voting rights can be granted denied via Oman Ghan Herbert-Blankson. Any voting rights must be included in the articles of organization via administrative change. If either principle  partner passes on the Kobena Robert Herbert-Blankson and Nenyi Essel Herbert-Blankson will inherit voting rights after age 30 unless Big A and Little A both expire then Kobena Robert Herbert-Blankson and Nenyi Essel Herbert-Blankson ownership converts to 50/50. Voting and management permissions are granted at age 40, 50, 60 in. Gifted stares to reduce tax liability.  


11. Oman Ghan Herbert-Blankson will make all financial decisions for OGHB Holdings LTD


12. OGHB Holdings LTD indemnifies Kobena Robert Herbert-Blankson and Nenyi Essel Herbert-Blankson against any legal proceedings/damages that may be occurred by OGHB Holdings LTD in the course of doing business.


13. All revenue from subsidiaries will passthrough child LLCs/ Baby up to OGHB Holdings LTD ( online courses, videos, blogs, etc but not limited too.)


14. OGHB Holdings LTD will receive most of its compensation as quarterly dividends minus expenses. Any members running subsidiaries ( operating companies) will receive compensation via W2 income and be set up on ADP payroll of another equal payroll processor.


15. OGHB Holdings LTD will absorb all profits and losses from subsidiaries for taxation purposes.



Hold on to the money in the company for one year. The take all money as quarterly dividends.


No partner single member entity then you would set this up as an S-Corp.
