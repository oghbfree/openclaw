Did some math / experiments & sharing the results:

The Hex number listed below the "Out" field is post the 4% withdrawl tax, and is exactly how much hex you'll get back in your hex / eth wallet.

To know your exact profit, take your “Total (Hex3)” under "My Vault" and paste it into the "Out" field. Subtract that number from your initial buy in, and thats how much additional Hex you've made. Use a percentage change / difference calculator to know the percentage growth.

tip 1: short stakes are better on hex3 because you will get hex3 at a cheaper rate compared to long stakes

tip 2: technically, you get "divs" through staking (by selling the hex3 earned from staking) or selling the inflated amount minus principal

tip3: stake your principal so you have your "divs" reflected under unstaked
