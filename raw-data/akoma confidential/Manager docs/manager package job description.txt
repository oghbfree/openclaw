### 2Real Enterprises (40% Time)

## CORE RESPONSIBILITIES
The operational backbone of 2Real Enterprises by ensuring all items, tools and equipment are sale-ready, accurately listed and efficiently fulfilled, directly supporting sales across Jiji, WhatsApp and the retail store.

Warehouse Operations:
  - Quick tidy/organization & sweep
  - Daily cleaning, test, maintenance, organization and photograph of products/tools/equipment
  - Anti-rust treatment for vulnerable tools/ products
  - Inventory management and stock tracking
  - Potential wholesale selling of warehouse goods at new location*

Order Enquiry & Fulfillment:
  - Collating customer enquiries for sourcing or fulfillment
  - Picking, packing, and logging deliveries for Jiji/WhatsApp/Zobase orders
  - Coordinating with delivery partners and updating tracking (Time Date| Customer | Items | Delivery Co. | Tracking #)

Digital Marketing:
  - Power Team Building from enquires: Pitch contractor and network
  - Managing social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
  - Daily WhatsApp status updates (5+ items with descriptions & prices)
  - Jiji listing creation with keyword-rich descriptions and customer response management
  - Upload listings to: WhatsApp Catalog, Jiji, Facebook Marketplace

### Akoma Robotics (60% Time)

CORE OBJECTIVE:
To ensure the operational excellence, scalability and financial integrity of the Company's robotics education programs through rigorous oversight, strategic client acquisition and effective team management.


Business Development:
  - School outreach and partnership acquisition
  - Conducting demonstrations for schools and parents using professional presentation equipment supplied
   - Effectively communicate the program's value proposition, benefits for students, and alignment with national STEM goals
  - Contract negotiation and finalization

Business Operations:
- Manage budgets and cost control

- Process payments and track finances

- Develop progress reports for management

- Identify and implement efficiency improvements

 Program Management:
- Student enrollment and registration processing
- Oversee the setup and logistics for all classes and camps.
- Course scheduling and facilitator coordination
- Equipment management and maintenance
- Organize and host culminating student competitions or showcase events to demonstrate learning outcomes and generate positive publicity.

Quality Assurance:
- Ensuring 100% equipment accountability primary enforcer of "The John Protocol," personally conducting all pre- and post-session equipment checks.
- Maintain the master equipment inventory, including serial codes, and ensure all kits are fully functional.
- Authorize and log all equipment movements, holding facilitators financially accountable for losses or damages.
- Monitoring course delivery standards
- Collecting and acting on student feedback

Facilitator Management & Training:
- Vet, recruit (alongside management) and train all facilitators on the Company's curriculum, teaching methodology, and operational protocols.
- Provide ongoing support, observation, and feedback to facilitators to ensure quality instruction.
- Manage facilitator schedules and commission payments via the Zobase time-tracking system.


Marketing & Promotional Oversight:
- Creating and posting 2+ social media posts per week for Akoma Robotics then 3+ after 4 schools onboard
- Oversee the development, distribution of promotional materials and managing enrollment campaigns
- Manage the student enrolment process and ensure smooth fee collection via the designated MTN Merchant account.
- Handle parent communications and inquiries
