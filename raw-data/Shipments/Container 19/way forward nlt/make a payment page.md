If NLT manage the property you are renting and you need to report a repair, please use the online reporting system.

This online system helps us to receive the information that we need in order to understand your problem.

It also allows you to upload photos of your problem which will help our contractors to bring the right tools and equipment to fix your problem.

To access the system please click here https://nltproperties.fixflo.com then bookmark/favourite the page on your smart phone, tablet or computer.

Repairs for non-emergencies that are reported online are likely to be dealt with more quickly than those that are reported by telephone or by email.

Emergencies must continue to be reported to us by telephone.
