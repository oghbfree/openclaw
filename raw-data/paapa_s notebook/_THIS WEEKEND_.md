ENTERTAINMENT:
Ghana Domestic Tourism Fair & Exhibition (GDTFE) 2013

Date:  Friday, September 20, 2013
To: Sunday, September 22, 2013

Venue: National Theatre Accra and Efua Sunderland Children’s Park, Accra Central

Organiser: Ghana Domestic Tourism Fair & Exhibition
Contact number: 0509040012 - 057 3134188
Email:  info@ ghfair.com
Website:  http://www.ghfair.com/

The Ghana Domestic Tourism Fair & Exhibition (GDTFE) 2013 will be on the Theme of ‘Growth & Employment’.

The aim of GDTFE is to promote Ghana’s Domestic Tourism industry and expose its potential to local and foreign investors.

The bi-annual three-day Fair & Exhibition seeks to:

• Provide a platform for expansion and opportunities for Ghana’s Domestic Tourism.

• Project domestic tourism as potential lead sector in the tourism industry for employment.

• Create awareness of what Ghana has to offer in domestic tourism.
• Network and create partnership with stake holders.
• Present Ghana's Tourist potential as a major investment portfolio

• Present Ghana’s competitive advantage in Africa in terms of trade Partnerships

• Expose existing trade and investment opportunities.
• Introduce the rich traditions and cultural heritage of the country.
