RE: APPLICATION FOR FOOD,DRUGS AND STANDARDS BOARD CERTIFICATION OF CHE MATE TEA

We hereby apply for Food and Drugs Authority registration and certifcation of Che Mate tea in the Republic of Ghana.

Since its introduction into the UK market, Che Mate tea has recieved tremenous patronage and has become popular in households across the UK. Sales have also increased as a result of its unique characteristic properties as a healthy tea.

We have enclosed copies of the certficate of origin, phytosanitary certificate (Parts 1 & 2) and the certificate of inspection for the import of products from organic production into the European community that was undertaken by the manufacturers to detrmine the chemical compostition of (Yerba Mate 'Ilex paraguariensis') Che Mate tea.
