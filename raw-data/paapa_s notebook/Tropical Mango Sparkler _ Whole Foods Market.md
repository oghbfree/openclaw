Ingredients:

- 1  cup fresh ripe mango
- 1  cup pineapple juice
- Juice of 1 lime
- Crushed ice
- 2  cups sparkling water or bottled fruit spritzer

You must be signed in to use shopping lists. [Sign in or create account](http://www.wholefoodsmarket.com/user/janrain_register)

Method:

Purée mango, pineapple and lime juice with crushed ice. Pour into glasses and top with chilled spritzer.
